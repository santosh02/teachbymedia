About us

who we are

we are Teach By Media

Founder Mr. Ranjeet Rajak and Co-founder Mr. Sudhir work with a talented team of professionals to accomplish goals and mission of Teach By Media.

Established in 2016, TEACH BY MEDIA (TBM) is a non-profit voluntary organisation. It works for the welfare and empowerment of underprivileged women, children, elderly and physically challenged individuals by enriching their lives and giving them access to education, health and livelihood skills.

Using media, technology and design thinking the projects goal was to discover how these new tools could enable NGOs to create more effective learning programs on the social challenges facing disadvanataged young people and their communities.

vision

Our vision is to nourish or polish the talent of every child so that they feel proud and special. Performing art is one of the effective way through which we can guide young heart of India to their way of success and to earn living hood through this joyful and talented way. Performing art plays a important role in human development, it has to be natured and encouraged. Performing art can help child in personality development, cognitive development, social and emotional development to improve aesthetic sense and child becomes tendacious and confident.

mission

Every child has a dream in their heart to be a best performer someday. Our mission is to providing platforms where hidden talent of slums/N.G.O children get a chance to perform or show their talent. We provide quality education and training related to performing arts like a painting, music, dance, theatre, singing, film making, sports, art and craft etc. We work for the upliftment of the poor children by providing child center training and education.

Our Way of Working

TEACH BY MEDIA has been helping children from urban/rural slums or N.G.O and from other communities to achieve success and earn bread butter for better life with the help of providing education and training in performing arts. Training of performing arts can help the child become tenacious, team oriented, problem solver who are confident and able to think creatively.
