Theatre - Training

Theatre is a collaborative form of fine art that uses live performers to present the experience of a real or imagined event before a live audience in a specific place, often a stage.

Dancing-Training

Dance is a performance art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement. This movement has aesthetic and symbolic value, and is acknowledged as dance by performers and observers within a particularculture. Dance can be categorized and described by its choreography, by its repertoire of movements, or by itshistorical period or place of origin.
